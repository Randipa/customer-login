Login and Sign up to Platform


The login and sign-up process is a crucial component of our platform. The process to sign-up typically involves providing basic information such as their name, email address, and a secure password. We take the security of our users' information seriously and employ industry-standard encryption protocols to safeguard their data.

Once the sign-up process is completed, users can proceed to the login phase. They will be prompted to enter their email along with their password to gain access to their account.

Upon successful login, in addition to browsing Knowledgebase and FAQs, users can access the ‘Submit a ticket’ option that allow users to directly contact us regarding any issues they may have. This enables our customer support agents will be able to provide personalized assistance to users regarding their issues. The users will also be able to access their profile and manage their account details. The login and sign-up process plays a pivotal role in creating a seamless user experience, enabling users to securely interact with our platform and maximize the benefits it offers.

Should users encounter any difficulties during the login or sign-up process, we provide comprehensive support resources. These resources may include step-by-step guides, frequently asked questions to address any concerns or technical issues promptly.

We strive to provide a user-friendly and secure login and sign-up experience, ensuring that users can effortlessly access our platform while maintaining the privacy and confidentiality of their personal information.

User Guide


Sign-Up:

  1. Visit e-Knowledge Customer Support System homepage.
  2. Look for the "login” button on top right corner.
  3. on it to initiate the sign-up process.
  4. Provide the required information, such as your name, email address, and a secure password.
  5. Once you've filled in the necessary details, click on the "Submit" button to complete the process.

Login:

  1. Visit our platform's homepage.
  2. Look for the "Login" button on top right corner.
  3. Click on it to go to the login page.
  4. Enter your registered email address in the designated field.
  5. Enter your password in the provided field. If you've forgotten your password, there should be a "Forgot Password" option that you can click on to initiate a password reset.
  6. Double-check the entered information for accuracy.
  7. Click on the "Submit" button to proceed.

Accessing Profile and Account Settings:

  1. After logging in, you'll typically find a user profile icon or a dropdown menu containing your username.
  2. Click on the profile icon or username to access your profile and account settings
  3. Explore the available options, which may include managing personal information, updating preferences, or changing account settings.
  4. Make any desired changes or modifications.
  5. Remember to save your changes if required.

Browsing the Knowledgebase and FAQs:

  1. Once logged in, look for a "Knowledgebase" or "FAQs" section in the navigation menu or on the homepage.
  2. Click on the "Knowledgebase" or "FAQs" link to access the resource.
  3. You'll typically find a search bar or categories to explore.
  4. If you have a specific question or topic in mind, enter relevant keywords in the search bar and click on the search button,
  5. Review the search results and click on the relevant articles or FAQs to find the information you need.
  6. If you prefer browsing by category, click on the relevant category to view a list of related articles or FAQs.
  7. Read through the articles or FAQs to find answers to your questions or to gain a deeper understanding of the topic.

Submitting a Ticket:

  1. If you can't find the information you need in the Knowledgebase or FAQs, you may need to submit a ticket for personalized assistance.
  2. After browsing the resources, if you still have an issue or inquiry, navigate to the support or contact section of our platform.
  3. Look for the "Submit a Ticket" option.
  4. Click on it to access the ticket submission form.
  5. Provide accurate and detailed information about the issue or inquiry you need assistance with.
  6. Attach any relevant documents or screenshots if necessary.
  7. Submit the ticket.
  8. Our customer support team will review your ticket and respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.


If you encounter any difficulties during the login or sign-up process or have further questions, refer to our comprehensive support resources, including the Knowledgebase and FAQs. These resources are designed to address common queries and provide helpful information.

We hope this updated user guide assists you in navigating our platform effectively. If you require additional support or have any further questions, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.

Managing User Accounts


Managing your user account on our platform gives you control over your personal information, preferences, and settings. Here's a guide on how to effectively manage your account:

Accessing Account Settings:

  1. After logging in, locate the user profile icon or a dropdown menu displaying your username.
  2. Click on the profile icon or username to access your account settings.
  3. Look for an option such as "Account Settings" or "Profile Settings" and click on it.

Updating Personal Information:

  1. Within the account settings, you will typically find a section to update your personal information.
  2. Review the existing information and make any necessary changes, such as your name, email address, or contact details.
  3. Ensure the accuracy of the updated information and save the changes.

Managing Privacy and Security:

  1. Explore the privacy and security settings within your account settings.
  2. Review the available options, such as privacy preferences or two-factor authentication settings.
  3. Adjust the settings according to your preferences and desired level of security.
  4. Enable two-factor authentication if available for an added layer of account protection.

Customizing Notifications:

  1. Look for a section related to notifications or communication preferences.
  2. Adjust the notification settings to your preferences, such as email notifications for updates, announcements, or ticket responses.
  3. Customize the frequency or types of notifications you wish to receive.

Reviewing Subscription or Membership Details:

  1. If applicable, navigate to the subscription or membership section within your account settings.
  2. Review your current subscription or membership details, such as plan type, billing cycle, or renewal date.
  3. Make any necessary changes, such as upgrading or downgrading your subscription level.
  4. Verify and update payment information if required.

Managing Password and Security:

  1. Locate the password or security section within your account settings.
  2. Follow the provided instructions to change your password if desired.
  3. Ensure you create a strong, unique password for account security.
  4. Consider using a password manager to securely store and manage your passwords.

Closing or Deactivating Account:

  1. If you wish to close or deactivate your account, look for an option within the account settings.
  2. Review the implications and consequences of closing your account.
  3. Follow the provided instructions to initiate the account closure process, if available.
  4. Keep in mind that closing your account may result in the loss of data, access to certain features, or services.

Remember to save any changes you make to your account settings by clicking on the "Save" or "Update" button, if applicable. If you encounter any difficulties while managing your account, consult our support resources or contact our customer support team for assistance.

By effectively managing your user account, you can ensure that your personal information is up-to-date, your preferences are customized, and your overall experience on our platform aligns with your needs and expectations.
